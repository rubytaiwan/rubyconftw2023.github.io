日本・松江市 ( Matsue City JAPAN)

Matsue City is situated to the north of Hiroshima and is the prefectural capital of Shimane Prefecture. Due to the fact that the creator of the programming language Ruby, Mr. Yukihiro Matsumoto lives in Matsue, the ‘Ruby City Matsue Project’ kick started in 2006 and since then Matsue has been known as the holy land of Ruby. The RubyWorld Conference that has been held in Matsue since 2012 brings together Rubyists from across the world. Likewise in 2006, Matsue City and Taipei City started exchanges due to the connections brought on by the flower of wealth, peony and since then every year, one can appreciate the peonies from Matsue at the Jianguo Flower Festival before the Chinese New Year.

Creator of Ruby

In 2014 Matsue City and Taipei City exchanged an MOU and in 2022,　with the addition of four cities from the surrounding region (Shimane Prefecture: Izumo City & Yasugi City; Tottori Prefecture: Yonago City & Sakaiminato City) signed a new MOU with Taipei City.

Moving forward, Matsue City and Taipei City would like to share the spirit of ‘Happy Hacking!!’ and deepen the ties through Ruby.

Matsue City Mayor Uesada & Taipei City Mayor Chiang at Jiangou Flower Market before Chinese New Year.
